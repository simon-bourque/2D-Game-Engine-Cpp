------------- Player -------------
W - Move forward
S - Move backward
A - Strafe left
D - Strafe right

Mouse X - Yaw player
Mouse Y - Pitch camera

-------------- Debug Camera --------
W - Move forward
S - Move backward
A - Strafe left
D - Strafe right

Mouse X + Right Mouse Button - Yaw camera
Mouse Y  + Right Mouse Button - Pitch camera


----------- Misc --------------------
ESC - Quit
F1 - Lock/unlock mouse cursor
F2 - Toggle fullscreen
F3 - Switch between player and debug camera
